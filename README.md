# AIFor21stCenturyKids: Bilingual AI Literacy Guide (English & Assamese)

> **"AI won't replace your child. But a child who directs AI will."**

Welcome to **AIFor21stCenturyKids**, a living Open Educational Resource (OER) designed to guide the next generation through the automated era. This bilingual platform (fully available in English and Assamese) provides parents and students aged 5 to 20 with a structured, step-by-step curriculum to develop foundational artificial intelligence literacy, digital safety awareness, and critical thinking.

---

## What is this platform about?

Our world is changing at an unprecedented pace. Rote memorization is no longer the key to success. In a world where AI can write code, draft documents, and generate art, the most valuable skill is learning to collaborate with, direct, and audit these cognitive systems.

This interactive book is divided into three age-appropriate tracks:
1. **The Explorers (Ages 5–9):** Introduces logical rule-making and teaches children how to distinguish between real human actions and deterministic machine behaviors.
2. **The Creators (Ages 10–14):** Focuses on prompt interaction techniques, understanding digital footprints, and recognizing bias in search recommendations.
3. **The Strategists (Ages 15–20):** Prepares young adults for the economic reality of automation, deepfake forensic detection, and the ethical responsibilities of deploying AI systems.

---

## Key Features

* **Fully Bilingual:** Seamlessly toggle between English and Assamese script. The reading fonts and spaces are custom-engineered to make Assamese ligatures highly readable.
* **Interactive Quizzes:** Every chapter concludes with a comprehension check to reinforce logic structures and key takeaways, providing instant feedback.
* **Lesson Progress Checklists:** Checkboxes mark your progress as you finish lessons, saving your status locally on your device without requiring complex login passwords.
* **Offline-Ready:** Works completely offline without an active internet connection. Designed for classrooms, schools, and remote locations with limited bandwidth.
* **Decoupled Outbound References:** High-quality external reading lists and source videos linked at the bottom of lessons to help you study topics further.
